Elite Dangerous

Elite Dangerous is a game that was released on December 16th, 2014.
It was developed and published by Frontier Developments and is one of my favorite
games of that year.

The goal of the game is to become a successful freelance pilot in the Pilot's Federation,
you can do a lot to make money, from slave trading to bounty hunting, there is plenty to do,
in this game. There is also a focus on exploration, you can go out from the inhabited area of the Milky
Way galaxy and explore nebulas, find black holes, and some have even found alien life-forms.


By the end of April 2015 Elite Dangerous had sold more than 500,000 copies.
